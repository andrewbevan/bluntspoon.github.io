Paper Plain is buzzing with excitement as we announce the launch of our first paper recycling centre. The official launch of this recycling centre is scheduled for Saturday 17 December 2016!

To celebrate this launch, we have decided to throw a bit of a party. Pop in anytime from 10am. There will be a paper recycling seminar and some fun paperless games. Join us as we try to break the record of the most paper recycled in one sitting!

On this day, you will also be able to receive double points for your recycling efforts. We can't wait to see you there!



We all love being rewarded right? That's why Paper Plain joined with Kulula airlines to give you the ultimate reward!

What does this partnership mean for you?

Because we love rewarding you for your recycling efforts. You can now receive up to 50% off flight vouchers from Kulula airlines, simply for recycling your paper waste.

Why Kulula?

This partnership was formed due to the fact that both Paper Plain and Kulula are passionate about saving the environment. We believe recycling is plain and simple, and we also believe in rewards. All we need is for you to partner with us and we can save the environment and you can get rewarded. It's a win win!




Paper Plain is a Durban based paper recycling company, who believes recycling starts in the home. We believe in sustainability and aim to create a future of 100% recycled paper in South Africa.

Only 59% of South African paper waste is recycled.(treevolution.co.za) We have the goal of pushing this number to 100% by 2020. We can't do this alone, that's why we need your help. We will even reward you for your recycling efforts.
