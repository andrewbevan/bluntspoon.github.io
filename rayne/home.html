Paper Plain is a Durban based paper recycling company, who believes recycling starts in the home. We believe in sustainability and aim to create a future of 100% recycled paper in South Africa.

Only 59% of South African paper waste is recycled.(treevolution.co.za) We have the goal of pushing this number to 100% by 2020. We can't do this alone, that's why we need your help. We will even reward you for your recycling efforts.
